About OBESystems

OBE Systems, Inc. has a history of quickly and competently developing proof of concept prototypes and then turning those prototypes into production designs. OBE Systems, Inc. products have received both commercial success and critical acclaim. Several designs can be found on the shelves of retail stores such as Fry's Electronics and CompUSA.


Diverse experience

  • Consumer electronics products
  • Medical handheld devices
  • Data acquisition and analysis
  • Broadcast quality video circuits
  • Telecommunications equipment
  • Wireless LAN software and site-survey tools
  • 3-D graphics programming
  • Electronic toys
  • Motion control
  • Virtual reality hardware and software
  • Home entertainment hardware and software

Embedded system development

  • All popular 8, 16 and 32 bit micro-processors
  • Realtime OS
  • User interface design
